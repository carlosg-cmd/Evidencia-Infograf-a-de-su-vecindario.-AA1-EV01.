🏠 Neighborhood Description Topic: Where I Live

Welcome to my neighborhood! This is a quiet and green place where I live.

My home is a two-story house with a gate, located on an unpaved street. There are many green trees in front of my house.

Several other houses are around my block.

The Ara supermarket is near my house, about 200 meters away.

There is a motorcycle workshop next to the street, five houses away.

Also, there is a large soccer field behind my house, where children play in the afternoons. I love my neighborhood!
